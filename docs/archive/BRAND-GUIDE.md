# Nexus AI - Brand Guide

## Brand Identity

**Name:** Nexus AI
**Tagline:** Your AI productivity partner
**Promise:** Speak naturally. AI does the rest.

## Design Principles (Resend-inspired)

1. **Minimalism First** - Remove until it breaks, then add back one element
2. **Typography Over Decoration** - Use scale and weight, not color
3. **Functional Beauty** - Every element serves a purpose
4. **Generous Whitespace** - Let content breathe
5. **AI Transparency** - Show what AI does, don't hide it

## Color Palette

```
Midnight (Backgrounds)
- midnight-950: #0A0A0F (body)
- midnight-900: #11111A (cards)
- midnight-800: #1A1A28 (elevated)
- midnight-700: #242438 (borders)

Electric (Primary Action)
- electric-500: #00D9FF (buttons, links)
- electric-600: #00B8D4 (hover)

Neutrals
- white: #FFFFFF (headings)
- slate-300: #CBD5E1 (body text)
- slate-400: #94A3B8 (secondary text)
```

## Typography

- **Font:** Inter (400, 500, 600 weights only)
- **Scale:** 14px body, 16px large body, 20px headings, 24px page titles
- **Line height:** 1.5 for body, 1.2 for headings

## Components Style

- **Buttons:** Solid colors, medium rounded (8px), no shadows
- **Cards:** Subtle borders, minimal backgrounds, generous padding
- **Inputs:** Simple borders, clear labels, inline validation
- **Icons:** Functional only, emoji preferred for personality

## Voice & Tone

**Do:**

- "AI understood your conversation"
- "Saved automatically"
- "Speak in German or English"

**Don't:**

- "Processing your request..."
- "Please wait while we..."
- "System is working..."

## AI Messaging

Show AI intelligence without being robotic:

- "AI is transcribing" (not "Transcribing...")
- "AI extracted 3 habits" (not "Data extracted")
- Simple confidence: "AI is confident" vs "Low confidence"
